Steps:

  1. Gather the Ingredients
  2. Heat Olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat
  3. Stir in chicken broth, tomato sauce and tomatoes
  4. Add pasta and cook until tender, about 10 minutes
  5. Stir in undrained beans and cook until heated
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
