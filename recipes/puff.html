Puff

Description:

This is puff

Ingredients:

  • 5 cups bread flour
  • 2 and half teaspoons salt
  • 2 cups water, or as needed
  • 2 cups unsalted butter, at room temperature

Steps:

  1. Mix flour and salt together in a large bowl or in the bowl of a stand mixer that is fitted with a dough hook.
  2. Place butter between two pieces of plastic wrap and pound into a flat disc using a rolling pin or other heavy object
  3. On a lightly floured surface, roll out dough into a large rectangle about 1/2 inch thick.
  4. Repeat this rolling, folding, and turning two more times, then refrigerate until firm.
